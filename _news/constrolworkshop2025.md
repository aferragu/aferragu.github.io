Next 5-6 Dec 2025, we are holding the international [Control Workshop Uruguay 2025](https://controlworkshop.uy), with leading researchers in the field! The workshop is organized by [Grupo MATE](https://grupo-mate.github.io) to honor [Fernando Paganini](https://controlworkshop.uy/about_fernando/) and his contributions to Control Theory.
